SmartKeep

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

SmartKeep does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Overview

SmartKeep is a personal productivity app that helps you organize goals and plan your day. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

SmartKeep stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your goals, milestones, and tasks
  • Your daily plan and task completion history
  • Your profile preferences (name, working hours, energy settings)
  • In-app notifications and reminders

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising data
  • No data sharing with third parties

SmartKeep has no backend server. There is no account system. Your data belongs entirely to you.

4. Internet Permission

SmartKeep requests internet access. This is a standard requirement of the Android WebView runtime that powers the app. The app does not make any network requests during normal use.

5. Notifications

SmartKeep sends local notifications to remind you of tasks and goals. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are never sent to external servers. You can disable notifications at any time in your Android device settings.

6. Data Security & Deletion

All data is stored in your device's private app storage. You can permanently delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app or going to Settings → Apps → SmartKeep → Clear Data on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

SmartKeep is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
